CanvasCatcher Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Summary: CanvasCatcher does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. CanvasCatcher does not collect personal information, analytics, usage data, device identifiers, or any other data from users.

What stays on your device

  • Photos you capture or import
  • Color palettes you create
  • App preferences and settings

This data lives only on your phone and is never transmitted anywhere. If you uninstall the app, this data is removed with it.

Network access

CanvasCatcher does not connect to the internet. The app does not include any network or telemetry code.

Permissions

  • Camera — used only when you tap to capture a photo within the app. Photos are saved locally.
  • Photo gallery — used only when you tap Gallery to import an existing photo, via Android's secure photo picker.
  • Vibration — used for optional haptic feedback when picking and saving colors.

Third-party services

None. CanvasCatcher does not use analytics platforms, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or any third-party SDKs that transmit data.

Children’s privacy

CanvasCatcher does not collect any data, including from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out via the developer contact email listed on the Google Play Store listing.
